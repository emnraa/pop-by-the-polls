Issues

issues being discussed

Environmentalism

Stein believes that we need a national comprehensive water plan, believing that "clean water is a human right."

Education

Stein has advocated for the abolition of abolishing the large amount of college debt that plagues many young people coming right out of university.

Foreign Policy

There is a refugee crisis that Stein believes is created mainly by US military interventions and Stein believes we need to take responsibility by taking in refugees.

Health Care

Steing stands for a Medicare-for-all system largely due to the fact that healthcare costs are spent on wasteful practices.

Presidential Candidate 2020

Jill Stein is the presidential candidate for the Green Party in the 2020 election.

Jill Stein

Jill Stein was also the Green Party Presidential candidate in 2012 and has taken part in the victories of campaign finance reform, racially-just redistricting, green jobs, and the cleanup of incinerators, coal plants, and toxic threats. She was also a main organizer for the Global Climate Convergence for People, Planet and Peace over Profit. She has many stances relating to environmentalism and a cleaner planet due to her exposure and vast knowledge on the links between toxic exposures and illnesses that she became aware of in 1990.
